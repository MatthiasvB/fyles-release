Fyles — Legal & Privacy

Fyles is a peer-to-peer file-sharing app. You create a link, and people send files straight to your device — there is no Fyles server in the middle, and your files and contacts never leave your devices except to travel directly to the peer you chose. Because there is no central server, there is no cloud account and nothing for us to collect.

This site hosts the legal and compliance documents for the Fyles app.

  • Data Deletion How to delete your data (spoiler: uninstalling removes everything).
  • Impressum Provider identification under German law (DDG §5).
